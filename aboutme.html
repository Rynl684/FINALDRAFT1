When was McDonalds Created?

McDonalds was created on April 15, 1955 in San Bernardino, California. It was founded by two brothers, Maurice McDonald and Richard McDonald. This is why McDonald is named McDonald. After a couple days after McDonalds was created, they made the Big Mac, Filet-O-Fish, and the Egg McMuffin. McDonalds was the first company to make a drive thru for their food and this passed on to many other fast food restaurants such as Burger King and In and Out.

Modern McDonalds

Currently in 2019, McDonalds is the most popular burger place in U.S.A.
